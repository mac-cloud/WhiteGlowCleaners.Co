Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide same-day service?
Yes! We offer same-day service within Nairobi for bookings made before 2 PM, subject to availability. Contact us via WhatsApp for immediate scheduling.
Do you bring your own cleaning supplies?
Absolutely! We bring all professional-grade cleaning supplies and equipment. You don't need to worry about purchasing anything - we've got it all covered.
How much do your services cost?
Our prices vary by service and package. Bedsitter cleaning starts from KSH 2,000, couch cleaning from KSH 2,500, and window cleaning from KSH 1,500. Click on each service above for detailed pricing.
Are you insured and bonded?
Yes, WhiteGlow Cleaners Co. is fully insured and bonded for your peace of mind. Your property and belongings are protected during our cleaning services.
Which areas in Nairobi do you serve?
We serve all areas within Nairobi County including CBD, Westlands, Karen, Kilimani, Lavington, Kileleshwa, and surrounding neighborhoods. Contact us to confirm service in your specific area.
How long does a typical cleaning session take?
Timing depends on the service: Bedsitter cleaning takes 2-3 hours, couch cleaning 1-2 hours, and window cleaning 1-3 hours depending on the number of windows. We'll provide an estimated timeframe when you book.
